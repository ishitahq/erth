# Dataset Class Labels

This dataset contains labeled samples of mixed plastic materials. Each label represents a category based on the dominant plastic type present in the sample.

## Class Definitions

* **mix PP**
  Mixed plastic waste where **Polypropylene (PP)** is the dominant material.
  Common examples include food containers, bottle caps, and packaging materials.

* **mix HD**
  Mixed plastic waste where **High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE)** is the dominant material.
  Typical items include detergent bottles, milk jugs, and household containers.

* **mix PET**
  Mixed plastic waste where **Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET)** is the dominant material.
  Often found in beverage bottles, food packaging, and transparent containers.

* **mix rigid**
  Mixed plastic waste composed primarily of **rigid plastics**, which may include a combination of polymer types but are characterized by their solid, inflexible structure.
  Examples include hard containers, crates, and molded plastic items.

## Notes

* The term "mix" indicates that samples may contain multiple plastic types, but are labeled according to the **predominant material or physical characteristic**.
* These labels are intended for classification tasks in recycling, waste sorting, or material identification applications.
* Additional unlabeled images are also provided in the dataset and can be used for purposes such as semi-supervised learning, validation, or further annotation.
